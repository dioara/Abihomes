Hale Barns Transformation

A stunning conversion of a 3-bed bungalow into a luxurious 6-bed house, showcasing expert design and construction.

Project Overview

This project involved the complete transformation of a modest 3-bedroom bungalow into a spacious and high-value 6-bedroom family home. Through innovative design and meticulous construction, we significantly increased the property's footprint and market value.

Initial State

3-Bedroom Bungalow

Final State

6-Bedroom House

Value Increase

£450,000 to £1,350,000

The transformation included significant structural changes, extensions, and a complete interior and exterior overhaul. Our team focused on maximizing space, light, and functionality while ensuring the highest standards of finish and durability, resulting in a property that tripled its original value.
